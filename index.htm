MagicSnap Android Privacy Policy

1. Photos and other camera output usages

This application requires access to your device camera. Permission is used only to allow you to take a photo that should be animated. Image is processed inside your device and never leave it (of course, you can share it manually, but our app won't do that). No external processing is used

2. Access to device file system

Similarly to camera access, sole purpose of this is to store your photos on your device and show you your photo galery. We won't read, write or send it anywhere. It will never leave your phone or other android device from this app.

3. Other personal information

We do not process any other personal information in any way. We do not gather any device telemetry.
